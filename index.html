The Story of Markiplier

Mark "Markiplier" Fischbach's journey to YouTube fame

Markiplier is known to give back to the community through his charity livestream events, where he typically plays games and raises money for a charity he's picked.

Some interesting facts about Markiplier:

  • Mark Edward Fischbach was born on June 28, 1989, in Honolulu, Hawaii
  • Mark enrolled at the University of Cincinnati, where he took a biomedical engineering program
  • In 2012, Fischbach was beset by many different issues, including a breakup, getting laid off from his job, getting kicked out from his house by his mom, and getting an emergency appendectomy and going into debt
  • Fischbach registered his first YouTube channel on March 6, 2012, originally intending to upload comedy sketches and action videos
  • Fischbach and Janet Varney co-hosted the 2015 South by Southwest Gaming Awards, and he was featured in YouTube Rewind
  • He was ranked sixth in a list of the twenty most influential celebrities among teenagers in the United States
  • On November 15, 2019, Fischbach launched a new channel with fellow YouTuber, Ethan "CrankGameplays" Nestor, called Unus Annus, a channel which planned to upload a video every day for one year, after which the channel would be deleted along with its videos
